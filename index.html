Temple of the Tooth

The Temple of the Tooth (Dalada Maligawa) perched on a hilltop overlooking the city. Walakada Purana Vihara, an ancient Buddhist temple complex with a seven-story stupa (dome-shaped structure) offering scenic views.

Botanical Garden

The Peradeniya Royal Botanical Gardens, located about 5.5 kilometers west of Kandy in the Central Province of Sri Lanka, is a captivating natural haven with a rich history dating back to the 19th century.Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, a photographer, or simply seeking tranquility, the Peradeniya Royal Botanical Gardens offer a delightful experience amidst the beauty of Sri Lanka’s flora and fauna

Hanthana Mountan

Hanthana Mountain Range | Katusukonda | Hiking in … WEBThe Hanthana Mountain Range belongs to the Kandy District of the Central Province. You can observe the entire range once you travel from Peradeniya to Delthota. Located in the southwest, it’s not far from Kandy City.
