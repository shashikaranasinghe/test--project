kandy city

Kandy is a major city in Sri Lanka, located in the central mountainous region. It's known for its rich history as the last kingdom of Sri Lanka and its cultural significance as a center for Buddhism.

Here are some interesting points to include about Kandy:

Founded in the 14th century as the capital of the Sinhala kingdom.
UNESCO World Heritage Site due to its historical and cultural significance
Home to the Temple of the Tooth (Dalada Maligawa), which houses a relic believed to be a tooth of the Buddha.
Peradeniya Royal Botanical Gardens, one of the largest botanical gardens in Asia
Kandy Esala Perahera, a vibrant annual procession showcasing Sri Lankan culture and traditions.
